KARNATAKA:

  • Cultural Heritage: Rich history with sites like Mysore Palace and Hampi.
  • IT Hub:
    • Home to Bengaluru, the "Silicon Valley of India."
  • Cuisine: Famous for its diverse and delicious cuisine, including Udupi and Mangalorean dishes.
  • Festivals: Celebrates vibrant festivals such as Dasara in Mysuru and Hampi Utsav.
  • Natural Beauty: Diverse landscapes including Western Ghats, Jog Falls, and numerous beaches.
