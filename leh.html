LEH-LADAKH:

  • Scenic Landscapes: Stunning Himalayan landscapes with high-altitude deserts, snow-capped peaks, and crystal-clear lakes like Pangong Tso and Tso Moriri.
  • Adventure Tourism: Popular destination for trekking, mountaineering, and river rafting in the Indus and Zanskar rivers.
  • Cuisine: Offers local Ladakhi cuisine and Tibetan dishes such as momos and thukpa.
  • Festivals: Celebrates cultural festivals like Hemis Festival with masked dances and vibrant celebrations.
  • Temples: Rich Tibetan and Buddhist heritage with monasteries.
